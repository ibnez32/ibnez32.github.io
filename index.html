Origins

  • Game of Thrones is a fantasy drama TV series created by David Benioff and D.B. Weiss.
  • It is based of the fantasy books “A Song of Ice and Fire” created by George R.R. Martin which began in 1996 and is still being published
  • George R.R Martin was inspired by historical events such as the Wars of the Roses in England and epic fantasy series such as “Lord of the Rings”.
  • The series premiered on April of 2011, and has been the most popular and highly rated show on television around the world ever since.

Summary

  • Game of thrones is an epic fantasy series that takes place in a fictional place called Westeros and Essos
  • Several great houses are fighting for control for the Iron Throne which controls the seven kingdom of Westeros which results in a war
  • An exiled previous ruling family (House Targaryen) is also scheming for the throne from Essos
  • At the same time an ancient evil is lurking in the far north of Westeros
